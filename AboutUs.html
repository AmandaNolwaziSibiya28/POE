About Us

Crowned by Amanda was founded with the intention of empowering, personalising, and democratising access to beauty. Initially, the brand offered individualised beauty consultations to help customers find routines, looks, and styles that suited their unique personalities and lifestyles.

Over time, through online content such as blogs, tutorials, and live sessions, the brand inspired and engaged a wider audience. In essence, Crowned by Amanda is committed to empowering individuals through lifestyle and beauty in order to promote confidence and self-worth.

Our target audience includes women aged 18–35 who value beauty, style, and confidence. We also support salon owners and hairdressers by providing quality, affordable hair products that build confidence daily.

Our Mission

To empower people by providing individualised beauty advice and consultations that improve wellbeing, self-expression, and confidence. Our goal is to make beauty approachable, encouraging, and customised to each individual’s lifestyle.

Our Vision

To establish a society where everyone feels inspired, respected, and self-assured via beauty and self-care, and to be a reliable partner in personal empowerment and transformation.
